What People Asked

FAQs

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions by our customers.

1. Which Time is the best for visiting Kerala?

Winter Season October to March is the ideal time to visit Kerala. At this time you get the best experience of KeralaNature by exploring beautiful landscapes, lush greenery, and serene backwaters, and best for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

If you visit Kerala for Ayurvedic treatments, then June to September (monsoon) is the ideal time. For Budget travelers, the Summer months (March to May) is the best time.

2. How much does a Kerala trip cost?

Kerala has many cities to explore and activities to do so the cost of a Kerala Trip depends on many factors including the duration of the trip, activities to do the type of accommodation, Food choice, and Transportation options. To get the best price for the Kerala Tour Package, we recommend reaching out to a PlanYourTour tour guide expert, They help you to create an ideal trip at an affordable price.

3. How many days are enough for Kerala?

Explore backwaters, beaches, hill stations, cultural heritage, wildlife, and green landscapes during the Kerala Trip, It takes 7 to 10 days, this is the ideal and the perfect duration for exploring the natural beauty of Kerala.

4. How do I plan a trip to Kerala?

Book your hassle-free Kerala trip, Contact PlanYourTour, for the best and ideal Kerala holiday package according to your interest and budget.

5. What are the best things to do on a Kerala trip?

Some popular activities to do in Kerala like exploring Munnar's Tea Plantations, Wildlife Safari in Periyar National Park, Kathakali Performance, Explore Kochi Fort, Ayurvedic Spa, Visiting the Backwaters of Kumarakom, Beach Relaxation in Kovalam, Exploring Wayanad's Natural Beauty, Houseboat Stay in Varkala, Visit Temples and Religious Sites and much more.

6. What can be expected from a Kerala trip?

Well, Kerala is a mixed bag of everything! Whether you are looking for honeymoon, adventure, leisure, beach, hill stations, or water destinations, you will find all your answers in Kerala.

7. What are the popular tourist activities in Kerala?

Kerala offers an array of activities to its visitors. To name a few, there are wildlife safaris, water sports, trekking, beach activities, hill station visits, cultural tours, bamboo rafting, and the list goes on!

8. Which is the best backwater destination in Kerala?

As you sort through Kerala tour packages, ensure it includes Kumarakom in your itinerary. Kumarakom is that dreamy backwater getaway that a typical picture postcard from Kerala is made up of.
